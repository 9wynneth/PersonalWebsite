About

I am currently in my fourth-semester of Information Systems for Business at Universitas Ciputra. Ever since I was a little I've wanted to be a doctor, a financial advisor, and even a lawyer. Never did I imagine that I would end up majoring in an IT field, and I am forever glad for having come across it. Coding has helped me grow so much in various aspects that I now somehow see the world with a different perspective. One thing that haven't changed though is my desire to impact the world for the better, to leave something behind that would be eternally helpful to anyone, and I know that through technology, I can make that happen.

I will say that I am quite active in college when it comes to volunteering for organizations and participating in events. I believe these experiences have helped me grow exceedingly as a person. First-hand experience is my preferred method to grow, and I have indubitably learnt a lot in aspects of communication, leadership, and teamwork.

I have a huge passion for sushi, crime shows, technology, and learning new things. This has led me to challenge myself every day to always do better and be better. Besides coding, I also have interests that lie in music (acoustic guitar), sports (basketball, soccer, badminton), as well as traveling (to city outskirts, outer countries, other nations, etc). I am always willing to branch out and learn more of people's culture and their food, especially.
